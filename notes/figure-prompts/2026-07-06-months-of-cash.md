# Figure prompts — 2026-07-06-months-of-cash

Two Illustrator "Text to Vector Graphic" assets. Workflow per
`notes/blog-authoring.md` §5: open the gear logo as the color source, run the
prompt, then add UPPERCASE condensed-sans captions in a type layer (the
generator drops free text).

Palette (from the gear logo): outlines `#143f33` / `#16221d`; teal `#2f7da3`,
lifted `#5b9fc0`, deep `#1c5572`; cream `#f7f1d7`, page `#f7f4ee`.

## Figure 1 — hero / OG card, `/images/2026-07-06-months-of-cash-hero.png`

**Canvas: 1200×630 landscape (1.91:1).** Referenced from front matter
(`figure:` and `og-image:`). No baked-in text.

Prompt:

> Flat vector illustration, bold uniform dark-green outlines, limited palette
> of teal blue, deep teal, cream, and dark green on a cream background. A wide
> horizontal scene: on the left, a large half-circle fuel-style gauge drawn as
> a bold outlined arc with tick marks, its needle pointing low into a small
> deep-teal danger wedge at the left end of the arc, the rest of the arc filled
> in lifted teal. The gauge sits on top of a simple outlined paper document
> with horizontal rule lines suggesting a balance sheet. On the right, a row of
> four outlined coin stacks of increasing height, each stack a short cylinder
> pile in teal with cream tops, standing on a thin baseline; the tallest stack
> at the far right has a small outlined shield leaning against it. Clean
> geometry, generous margins, icon style, no text, no gradients, no shadows.

Type layer afterwards (uppercase condensed sans, ink `#143f33`):
- Above the gauge: `MONTHS OF CASH`
- Under the danger wedge: `< 3 MO`
- Under the coin stacks: `THE RESERVE`

## Figure 2 — in-body, `/images/2026-07-06-months-of-cash-barbell.png`

Two-panel side-by-side scene (house layout #2), landscape. Illustrates the
"barbell" finding: NFF 2025 (small orgs: 52% at ≤3 months, 18% at ≤1 month)
vs BDO 2024 (large orgs: 62% above 7 months). All numbers go in the type
layer, not the generator prompt.

Prompt:

> Flat vector illustration, bold uniform dark-green outlines, limited palette
> of teal blue, deep teal, cream, and dark green on a cream background. Two
> equal panels side by side separated by a thin vertical rule. Left panel: a
> small outlined storefront-style building standing on thin cracking ice drawn
> as a cream floe with angular crack lines over deep-teal water, a single
> small coin beside the door. Right panel: a large outlined institutional
> building with columns standing on a solid layered plinth of stacked stone
> blocks, a tall neat stack of coins beside it. Both panels share a common
> horizon line. Clean geometry, generous margins, icon style, no text, no
> gradients, no shadows.

Type layer afterwards (uppercase condensed sans, ink `#143f33`):
- Panel heading, left: `MOST NONPROFITS — 52% HOLD ≤ 3 MONTHS (NFF 2025)`
- Panel heading, right: `THE CAPITALIZED TOP END — 62% HOLD > 7 MONTHS (BDO 2024)`
- Centered above both: `THE RESERVES BARBELL`
